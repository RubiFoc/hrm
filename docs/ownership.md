# Documentation Ownership

| Area | Owner Role | Review Cadence | Trigger for Immediate Update |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `project/` | business-analyst | monthly | scope or KPI change |
| `architecture/` | architect | monthly | interface/schema/infra/data-flow decision |
| `operations/` | coordinator | monthly | incident or release process change |
| `testing/` | tester | bi-weekly | test policy or critical bugfix |
| `llm/` | coordinator | bi-weekly | agent workflow or template change |
| `engineering/` | architect | monthly | best-practice policy update |

## Review Rules
- Each update must include date and editor.
- Stale section threshold: 45 days without review.
