# Legal Framework (Belarus + Russia)

## Last Updated
- Date: 2026-03-04
- Updated by: architect

This list contains core legal acts for HRM processing of personal data and information security.

## Belarus: Priority NPAs
- Law of the Republic of Belarus dated 07.05.2021 No. 99-З "On Personal Data Protection".
- Law of the Republic of Belarus dated 10.11.2008 No. 455-З "On Information, Informatization and Information Protection".

## Russia: Priority NPAs
- Federal Law dated 27.07.2006 No. 152-ФЗ "On Personal Data".
- Federal Law dated 27.07.2006 No. 149-ФЗ "On Information, Information Technologies and Information Protection".
- Federal Law dated 21.07.2014 No. 242-ФЗ (amendments introducing personal data localization requirements for Russian citizens).
- Government Decree of the Russian Federation dated 01.11.2012 No. 1119 "On approval of requirements for personal data protection in personal data information systems".

## Application Notes
- These acts are baseline requirements for product design, data storage, and security controls.
- For production launch, legal review must map exact article-level obligations to implemented controls.

## Compliance Scope for This Project
- Jurisdiction scope: Belarus and Russia only.
- Data categories with highest sensitivity: candidate and employee personal data, interview data, HR records.
